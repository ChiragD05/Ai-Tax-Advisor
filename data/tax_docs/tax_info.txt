Section 80C allows deductions up to Rs. 1.5 lakh for investments such as ELSS, PPF, and LIC.

HRA exemption is available under Section 10(13A) for salaried individuals.

Under the new tax regime, most deductions including 80C are not allowed.

Section 80D provides deductions for health insurance premiums.

Section 24 allows deduction on home loan interest.
